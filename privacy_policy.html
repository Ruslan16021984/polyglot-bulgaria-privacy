Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

The “Poliglot Bulgaria” app respects user privacy. This policy describes what data may be used by the app.

What data is used

The app may store the following data locally on the user's device:

  • lesson progress;
  • exercise results;
  • saved words and the user's dictionary;
  • app settings.

This data is stored only on the user's device and is not shared with third parties.

In-app purchases

The app uses Google Play Billing to unlock the full course. Payment processing is handled by Google Play. The app developer does not receive access to the user's bank card or payment details.

Voice input

Some exercises may use the Android voice input feature. Voice processing is handled by Google services in accordance with their privacy policy.

Data sharing

The app does not sell or share users' personal data with third parties.

Children

The app is intended for educational purposes and does not contain harmful content.
